About Me

I am an undergraduate Data Science student with hands-on experience in Python, SQL, and machine learning. During my internship at PT. IMS, I deepened my SQL expertise while also exploring C# and Highcharts, contributing to client-facing dashboards that transformed financial data into actionable insights. That experience solidified my passion for working with data—shaping raw information into meaningful solutions that support decision-making.

I aspire to grow into a Data Scientist or Data Engineer, roles where I can apply both technical and analytical skills while continuously learning. I actively seek improvement in both hard and soft skills, and my long-term goal is to work in a multinational company, eventually pursuing opportunities abroad as an expat.

Outside of academics and work, I enjoy playing games—mainly osu!—and love listening to music, especially C-Pop. I’m also a fan of sports like badminton and (casually) basketball, and I enjoy reading, whether it’s fiction to unwind or psychology to better understand people and ideas. These hobbies keep me balanced and fuel my curiosity, both in and out of the data field.
